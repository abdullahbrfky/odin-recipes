Ground Beef Crockpot Lasagna

This easy meal is perfect for busy weeknights or a slow Sunday.

    Ingredients

  • 2 lbs ground beef
  • 2 24oz cans of spaghetti sauce
  • 2 bottles of alfredo sauce
  • 1 large bag of mozarella cheese
  • 1 package of no bake lasagna noodles
  • Â½ cup of chopped onions
  • 2 cloves of chopped garlic

    Directions

  1. Brown ground beef in a pan
  2. Add in chopped garlic and onion
  3. Mix in the two 24 oz cans of spaghetti sauce with the ground beef. Reserve 1/2 cup to place in the bottom of the crock pot.
  4. Pour 1/2 cup sauce into bottom of crock pot
  5. Add layer of no bake lasagna noodles. Break them to create an even layer of noodles around the crock pot
  6. Add layer of ground beef and spaghetti sauce mixture
  7. Add a layer of alfredo sauce
  8. Add a layer mozzarella cheese
  9. Repeat three times (or until your crock pot is full)
  10. Make sure your last layer is noodles, then ground beef sauce then mozzarella cheese
  11. Slow cook for 4-6 hours or 8 hours if you want the edges crispy
